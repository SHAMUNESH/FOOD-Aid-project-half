How does FOOD-AID work? How can you Donate?


Create Account

Step 1 : Create your FOOD-AID Donor Account and build your profile. We have got you amazing things lined up once you join our proud Donor Fam

Submit Form

Step 2: Once you ready with your Account, Click on the Donate Button in your Dashboard. Fill up the Donate Form carefully with specifying the Food Details and location to get your Food.

Time to Donate

Step 3: Once you placed your Donate Order,Our Volunteer SuperHero will be on the Spot right away to collect the Food! And That's it :)

Updates

Step 4: Our Team will make sure that your Food goes to the right people. After all, You will be receving notification and you can see updates on your Dashboard. Hurrayy!!
